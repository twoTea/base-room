What is JustScoot Credit Score?

Alike other credit scoring systems, JustScoot Credit Score evaluates the user’s riding etiquette while using JustScoot services in order to improve the quality of rides. The Credit Score is generated from trip data such as ride time and parking etiquette.

How it works

Upon registration, users will be given 100 points and will be adjusted monthly based on the user’s riding etiquette.

Points Addition:

  1. -Park in designated parking areas
  2. -Riding within area of operation
  3. -Obey traffic regulations
  4. -Reporting of damaged bikes or illegal parking
  5. -Providing feedbacks

Points Deduction:

  1. -Violation of traffic rules
  2. -Damaging JustScoot Scooters
  3. -Fail to lock JustScoot Scooters after riding
  4. -Riding outside area of operation
  5. -Park outside designated parking areas

*There may be additional charges in the event users’ Credit Score falls below 50
